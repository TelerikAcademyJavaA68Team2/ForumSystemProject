About BeerTag!

BeerTag is a web application designed to help beer enthusiasts discover new beers and learn more about their favorite brands.

Our mission is to:

  • Provide detailed information about beers, including their name, alcohol by volume (ABV), and style.
  • Create a platform where beer lovers can explore and share their experiences.

We hope you enjoy using BeerTag and find it useful in your beer exploration journey!
